About Hello Bakery

Welcome to Hello Bakery Birmingham, where we craft delicious pastries, cakes, cookies, and small batch ice creams with love and the finest ingredients. Located in the heart of Crestline Village, we're your neighborhood bakery for all things sweet and fresh.

Every item is made fresh daily, ensuring you get the best quality and taste in every bite. From our flaky croissants to our custom celebration cakes, we're here to make your day a little sweeter.
